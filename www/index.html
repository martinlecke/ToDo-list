Add items

You can add items by typing in the textfield and either press the button Add Item or press Enter-key.

Move items

All items you can move up or down. Items in top of the list and bottom of the list dont have buttons for getting any higher up and lower in the list.

Delete items

By hovering over the specific item there is a menu popping up, by pushing the trashcan icon you remove the item.

Complete items

By pressing the left square, you mark the current item Completed and the square turns green.

Done
Move to done

When there is completed items in your to do list, a button turns up in the bottom of the list. By pressing Push completed tasks to Done you move completed items to your Done list.

Empty Done list

To clear your done list from old completed task press the Trash can at top of the done list.
